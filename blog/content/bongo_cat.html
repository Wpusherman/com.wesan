Today, I want to introduce "Bongo Cat," a game I've been playing non-stop lately!

This game is a clicker where this cat "bongo" (pats,) counting both your clicks and keystrokes. And yes, the fact that it counts your keystrokes is what's so revolutionary. Do you often type thousands of characters for some tasks? Well, in my case, I often do while writing a script of my vid or summarising book content I read, but... keystrokes are the basis when you use a computer; everyone does it. So you can rack up clicks easily!

If you love to unlock all the achievements of a game, you might have played lots of clicker games a while back. Some of them have one achievement at all, and you can unlock it if you click only once. Like the one below, for instance.

This game also follows the style of what some might call 'NFT games.'(?) So every 30 minutes you can spend 1,000 clicks to receive a cosmetic item. Thus, you may not like this game if you hate such a game.

Having said that, I think it's a well-made clicker game, which is why I have such a positive impression of it. If not, I wouldn't be praising it this much.

BTW, there was a update on 8.11, and you can enjoy Meowtiplayer! You can type or click with up to 100 players. Unfortunately, I have no friends to play this together, so I have never tried multiplayer.

Well, trading items happens quite frequently. You may be able to get some Steam Wallet by selling the skins.

As of this writing, I have clicked over 80K times. I need to click 1M times to unlock the final achievement, so I'll keep at it.

So, that's all of my introduction of Bongo Cat. Give it a try! I've started a Steam Curator page. I'll post the translated version on this blog, but since it seems you can do various things, please follow me! Seeya!
